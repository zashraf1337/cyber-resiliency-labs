# CR Labs — About page (paste into Substack → Settings → About)

**CR Labs makes modern AI legible.**

I'm Zubair Ashraf. I've spent nearly twenty years in security and engineering — vulnerability research,
red-teaming, threat intelligence — and I now spend my time on AI: post-training and aligning open models, and
explaining how these systems actually work.

This newsletter is the written companion to that work. Expect clear, jargon-light explainers on how large
language models are built, trained, shrunk, and run; honest pieces on the business and the careers around AI;
and the occasional security teardown from the archives. No hype, no hand-waving — just the real mechanics,
written for a smart person who doesn't already live inside the field.

The full library, with all the diagrams and code, lives at **crlab.ca**. The videos are on
**[YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCQ1PDMYSh8m6vvw_57r52Tw)**. Subscribe here to get each new piece
by email.

money and the risk sit. And, now and then, a security teardown from twenty years of doing this work.
