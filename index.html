AI training, education & security

We help organizations post-train AI models, and we make AI easier to understand.

Cyber Resiliency Labs and Consulting is an Ontario firm. We fine-tune and align open language models for businesses, non-profits, and mission-driven teams. We make hard AI topics clear through explainer videos, and we research the security of AI systems.

What we do

Model post-training

We fine-tune and align open language models: supervised fine-tuning, preference optimization (DPO and RLAIF), and the data pipeline behind them. You get a model shaped to your domain and values, with the improvement measured, not asserted.

AI education

We explain dense research and hard AI topics in plain language, as videos and training. We already produce walkthroughs that make new methods understandable to non-specialists, so your team learns what a model does and why.

AI security research

We red-team AI systems and research vulnerabilities in AI products, from model behavior to the infrastructure around it.

How we work

Small, senior, research-driven

A focused team that does the work itself. We stay close to the current literature and bring methods from research into production.

Open models, measured results

We work with open weights so you keep control of your model and data. We measure before and after, so an improvement is a number you can check.
