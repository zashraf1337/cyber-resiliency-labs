Cyber Resiliency Labs and Consulting is an Ontario research and consulting firm. We fine-tune open language models to a team's domain and values, and we research the security of AI products.

What we do

Model post-training

We fine-tune and align open language models: supervised fine-tuning, preference optimization (DPO and RLAIF), and the data pipeline behind them. You get a model shaped to your domain and values, with the gain measured, not asserted.

Evaluation & benchmarking

We build benchmarks and automated judging to measure how a model actually behaves, including under pressure. This includes value-alignment evaluation against published benchmarks.

AI security research

We red-team AI systems and research vulnerabilities in AI products, from model behavior to the infrastructure around it.

How we work

Small, senior, research-driven

A focused team that does the work itself. We stay close to the current literature and bring methods from research into production.

Open models, measured results

We work with open weights so you keep control of your model and data. We measure before and after, so an improvement is a number you can check.
